Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the subtitles?

Our High Accuracy model averages 98%+ accuracy for clear audio. It handles background noise and accents effectively, though highly complex technical terms may require a quick proofread.

What file sizes are supported?

You can upload files up to 2GB in size. For massive video files, we recommend exporting an audio-only version (like MP3) to speed up upload times.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Files are processed on secure servers and automatically deleted immediately after the subtitle generation is completed. We do not store or use your media for training.

How long does processing take?

Typically, processing takes about 10-15% of the file's duration. A 10-minute video will usually generate subtitles in around 1 to 1.5 minutes.

Which languages are supported?

We support over 90 languages including English, Tamil, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Arabic, and many more for both transcription and translation.
